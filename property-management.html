Property Management

Tenant Services

The FRP property management department manages more than 2.5 million square feet of industrial, warehouse and office space providing services to over 40 companies spanning from Newark, Delaware to Norfolk, Virginia.

At FRP, we believe quality service is the foundation of successful property management and tenant retention. Complete tenant satisfaction is our highest priority. Backed by the resources and experience of a proven team of professionals, we focus on the needs of our tenants in conjunction with effective and efficient property operation.

If you need to reach the property management department to report an issue or discuss a concern, there are several methods you may use to reach us:

  • Call 410-771-8143 - this goes directly to the Property Management Department
  • print this form (PDF) and send it via email or fax to 410-771-8150
  • Fill out this online contact form.

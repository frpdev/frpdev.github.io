Port Capital Center

7700 Port Capital Drive, Elkridge, MD

120,758 square feet of warehouse

Port Capital Center in Howard County, Maryland contains 6.39 acres with 120,758 square feet of warehouse inclusive of a 91,218 footprint and 29,540 square feet of office space. The property is zoned M-1 (Manufacture Light) with a CAC (Corridor Activity Center) overlay zone.

General Information

  • Located in the heart of the Baltimore-Washington Industrial Corridor
  • Direct access to Interstate 95 and MD 175
  • Highly functional distribution center
  • Rear-loaded building featuring 24 clear ceilings
  • Over 1.3 spaces per 1,000 parking
  • Fully-sprinklered
  • Truck court depth of 110 feet
  • Building leased through October 2025

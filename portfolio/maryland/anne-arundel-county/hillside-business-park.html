Hillside Business Park

7001-7030 Dorsey Rd Hanover, MD 21076

567,473 square feet

Hillside Business Park in Anne Arundel County, Maryland consists of 49 usable acres.  Four warehouse/office buildings and one suburban office building totaling 567,473 square feet exist on the property.  Hillside is located in the heart of the BWI Airport Business District at the intersection of Route 100 and the Baltimore-Washington Parkway (I-295.  The park enjoys quick and easy access to I-95 (via Route 100), the primary north-south interstate in the dynamic Baltimore-Washington Corridor and Mid-Atlantic region.  The park provides many close-by amenities and is approximately 1.5 miles from the Arundel Mills Mall, a 1.5 million square feet regional mall and entertainment complex including multiple restaurants, shopping and banking establishments. 


The MTA #17 bus line stops at the Arundel Mills Mall and the Marc/Camden Line, offering service to Baltimore and Washington is located in the Dorsey Business Park approximately 5 miles west of Hillside.  The W-1 (Light Industrial) zoning permits a wide variety of uses in manufacturing, processing, and warehousing consistent with an investment-grade business park.  Architectural control has been maintained throughout the development of Hillside in order to preserve the integrity of the business park.  Electric and natural gas service to Hillside are provided by Baltimore Gas & Electric Company (BG&E).  Public water and sewer are supplied by Anne Arundel County. Telephone service, with state-of-the-art fiber optics availability, is provided by Verizon.

General Information

  • 49 usable acres
  • Located in Anne Arundel County at the intersection of Baltimore-Washington Parkway (Route 295) and Route 100
  • Easy access to the Baltimore-Washington International Airport (2 miles) and Train Station
  • Near hotels, Arundel Mills Mall, restaurants, cafes and the MARC train station
  • Located within five miles of major employment centers (Ft. Meade and Columbia) & educational facilities (UMUC, John Hopkins, UMBC) via I-95 or MD-295

Leasing

FRP Development prides itself on the ability to customize leasing programs to meet the needs of growing organizations.

A detailed and fully transparent approach to leasing is one of the main factors that has led to FRP's ongoing growth and success. Our buildings have been built or acquired in prime locations that foster and encourage business growth for our tenants. Our in-house leasing team strives to create relationships with both customers and the larger brokerage community to manage the complete leasing process for commercial properties.

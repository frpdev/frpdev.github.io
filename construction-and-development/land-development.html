Land Development

FRP’s Land Development expertise allows our team to accurately and efficiently perform feasibility studies and assess development costs for large complicated parcels. 

Our knowledge in public infrastructure, storm water management, reforestation and afforestation, grading, subdivision, and zoning provide the tools required to transform large, raw, and non-entitled land parcels to sub-divided, cleared, and entitled lots, ready for vertical construction with all storm water management and utilities in place.  FRP’s goal is to leverage our land development abilities to increase land value and maximize development opportunities, while using best design practices and being mindful to political, social, and environmental requirements.  The ability to work with many different counterparts throughout the development process, including engineers, government agencies, surveyors, attorneys, and leasing agents, has provided FRP the ability to convert ideas into real property for both internal development and sale to third-parties.
